Art

Since the age of sixteen I have been interested in art. My favorite mediums to work with are charcoal, acrylic paint, and lineoleum printing. Being a creative is one of the things that excites me about life the most, that we have the ability as humans to take a concept in our minds and bring it to life.

Music

Music brings me inspiration and joy, no matter what mood I am in, I can always find a song or piece of music to reflect that state. Electronic music and dancing in different cities all over the world is a continued interest of mine. Living outside of New York City has given me an eclectic appreciation for all different types of music.

Family

Family has always been an important part of my life. My brothers and sister have always been a constant support and source of joy. What matters most to me is building relationships with the people I love most. Creating memories together with my family and friends continues to enrich my life.

Nature

At the age of thirty six I have traveled to over thirteen different countries. The beauty of nature and the diversity continues to expand my mind and heart. There are no troubles when I experience being outside in the fresh air. Hiking and swimming are my most favorite activities in Nature. Long Island continues to be one of my favorite places to enjoy nature even with my extensive traveling, nothing compares to home.
